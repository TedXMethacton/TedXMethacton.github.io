Gail Martin

Gail Martin currently works in the Marketing Department of Reed Tech, a LexisNexis company, helping to develop leads and spread the word about the revolutionary and innovative software technology available to help patent lawyers craft winning patent strategies. She has worked for more than 25 years in all major areas of communications program strategy (traditional media, social medial channels, fundraising and development communications and events, webinars, video and digital communications) as both practitioner and administrator. She has extensive experience with non-profit communications planning and working with global partners, more recently working in the B2B arena. She is a graduate of Shippensburg University with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication and holds a Certificate in Non-Profit Executive Administration from the University of Pennsylvania.
